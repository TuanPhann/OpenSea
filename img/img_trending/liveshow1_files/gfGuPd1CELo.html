OpenSea is the largest peer-to-peer marketplace for digital goods, including game items, collectibles, event tickets, domain names, and digital art. On OpenSea, users can buy and sell NFTs via Ethereum smart contracts using a variety of auction mechanisms, like eBay-style auctions, bundles, dutch auctions, and fixed-price listings. We also partner with developers to build customized marketplaces for their NFT projects and are currently the primary marketplace for many top blockchain games.
